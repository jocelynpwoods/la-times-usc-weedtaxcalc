Are you spending double what your neighbors are on pot tax?

By ALYSSA LYON, JOCELYN WOODS

April 18, 2018

Thirteen miles south of downtown Los Angeles, in the city of Carson, more than half of voters approved an 18 percent tax on all cannabis-related businesses. In the neighboring town of Culver City, that same tax is set at 0 -- for now.

This means if a customer were to purchase $100 worth of recreational weed in Carson, they would pay almost double the tax than if they were to buy it just 19 miles away.
